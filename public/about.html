Our Story

Eventify started when our founders struggled to plan a family wedding—finding reliable vendors, staying within budget, and aligning schedules took weeks. We believed technology could do better. Today, Eventify brings AI-powered recommendations, real-time availability, and seamless booking to anyone planning an event.

What We Offer

  • AI-powered recommendations based on your budget and preferences
  • Real-time vendor availability and instant booking
  • Secure payments and transparent pricing
